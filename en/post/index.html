About Esaka Sensei Resigned

Dear members. Esaka Sensei was resigned from his post as the Representative Director of World MJER Iaido Federation on May 30, 2021. He said “Because of I’m getting older, and my strength declining”. Then, Esaka sensei designated Kobara sensei(Chairman of Genshin-Kan) as new representative Director. And we decided to new representative Director of World MJER Iaido Federation is Kobara sensei.
